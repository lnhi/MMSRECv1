Title: Jewelry Silver Dip Cleaner 8 oz. ; Description: Simply Place Jewelry into jar, immerse for 2 minutes. Rinse with hot water and wipe clean with a soft lint free cloth. Can also be applied with a soft cloth to larger items. Do only 1/2 of the large item and rinse before continuing. Avoid using on pearls, opals, coral, some genuine stones, painted or enamelled surfaces. ;
